About the Role
We are seeking an experienced AI Product Manager to lead the strategy,
development, and rollout of agentic AI capabilities across Pacvue. This role is ideal
for a builder who can translate ambiguous opportunities into production-ready
products - from problem framing and solution design to testing, launch, and
iteration. You will work closely with engineering, data science, design, GTM, and
business teams to deliver safe, useful, and differentiated AI experiences for
eCommerce and advertising workflows.
Responsibilities
• Own the product vision, roadmap, and execution for agentic AI initiatives,
identifying where autonomy, reasoning, and workflow orchestration can create
meaningful customer value.
• Lead end-to-end development of agentic AI solutions, including opportunity
sizing, requirements, prototyping, evaluation design, testing, launch readiness, and
post-launch iteration.
• Partner closely with engineering, data science, design, and domain teams to
build safe, scalable, and high-performing AI products and systems.
• Define clear user problems, jobs to be done, success metrics, and guardrails
for AI features, balancing innovation with trust, reliability, and business impact.
• Drive cross-functional alignment across product, engineering, research, legal,
security, marketing, sales, and customer-facing teams to move initiatives from
concept to adoption.
• Work hands-on with teams to shape prompts, tools, model selection, retrieval
strategies, orchestration patterns, and human-in-the-loop workflows where
appropriate.
• Establish rigorous evaluation and testing practices for agentic systems,
including offline evals, online experiments, failure-mode analysis, and quality
monitoring.
• Stay current on the latest developments in AI, including LLMs, agents,
reasoning models, tool use, benchmarks, and infrastructure, and translate relevant
advances into product strategy.
• Leverage Pacvue's data and workflow context to create differentiated AI
experiences that improve productivity, decision quality, and campaign or commerce
outcomes.
• Partner with customers and internal stakeholders to understand pain points,
gather feedback, and ensure strong adoption of AI-powered features.
